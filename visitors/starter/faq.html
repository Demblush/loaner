Faq

Loanmedium is a Member-to-Member Lending Platform that connects lenders & borrowers.

Visit our homepage: https://loanmedium.com Once you are there, scroll down and click on Try now or on the menu icon, and navigate to Register.

If you have clicked on the "Get Started" or "Register" and you get a blank page, it means your browser might not support our code. What you have to do is to switch browsers to either FireFox or Google Chrome.

Please note that Loanmedium is only available in Nigeria. You can only use a Nigerian Phone number to register. When entering your phone number, do not include spaces or +234. Instead use the 080 format.

The security check is meant to confirm that it's actually a human that is browsing our site, not a bot. To perform the security check, enter only the numbers of the color required of you, only then will the submit button be clickable.

If you haven't received your SMS Verification Code, only request for the code after waiting for 1 hour. If you are still having problems, it means DND (Do Not Disturb) has been enabled on your sim card, and you won't be able to receive bulk sms messages on your sim card.

If you have forgotten your password, go to the Login page and click on Forgot Password.

Navigate to the Transactions page and click on Activate. Then select the stage you want to join. Once you've done that, the transaction will be pending till you are paired with the member you will make payment to.

Don't be scared. You are on the right track & this is normal. It means there's no available spot for now. Once there is an available spot, you will be paired to make payment. Once you are paired, you will see the bank account to make payment into, and other instructions.

Make payment into the account number that would be shown on your Transactions page. After making the payment, click on "I Have Paid". By doing this, you are confirming that you have paid and the person you paid to would also do the same.

Go to your Transactions page and click on Withdraw. If you are on a level and you don't have a downline, you will be asked to bring at least 1 downline to be able to withdraw. You need 1 active downline PER withdrawal.

Go to your Transactions page and click on Withdraw. If you don't have a downline, you will be asked to bring at least 1 downline to be able to withdraw. This is only required on level 1. Level 2 upwards doesn't require this. You need 1 active downline PER withdrawal. That means if you have 1 active downline, you will be able to withdraw only N1,000. If you have 2 active downlines, you will be able to withdraw only N2,000. If you have 3 active downlines, you will be able to withdraw only N3,000. If you have 4 active downlines, you will be able to withdraw only N4,000.

Level 1: Pay N1,000 to get N4,000, you can only withdraw N1,000 at a time.

Level 2: Pay N2,000 to get N6,000, you can only withdraw N2,000 at a time.

Level 3: Pay N5,000 to get N15,000, you can only withdraw N5,000 at a time.

Level 4: Pay N10,000 to get N30,000, you can only withdraw N10,000 at a time.

Level 5: Pay N20,000 to get N60,000, you can only withdraw N20,000 at a time.

Click on "I Have Paid" and upload the proof of payment. Proof of payment is a picture that shows you have made the payment. It could be a debit or email alert.

If you are unable to get a proof of payment for one reason or the other, simply upload a picture of yourself. There won't be any repercussion for doing this as long as the person you paid to confirms you.

If you haven't activated your account or do not have an active downline, you WON'T be able to withdraw. In order to get an active downline, please invite your friends to Loanmedium using your referral link or ID. Once they acivate their account (once they pay), they will become your active downline.

Once the member's deadline for payment expires, please click on "Not Paid", then click on Withdraw again, so that you can be paired with another member. Note: if you fail to click on "Not Paid", the transaction will be automatically confirmed within 72 hours. And there is no refund.
